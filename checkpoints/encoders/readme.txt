In this folder are stored the checkpoints of encoders. 
The name describes the hyperparameters used and the number of classes.
They are:

P*.*_E*

Where P is for "(hyper)Parameters" and the two integers that follow indicates the first two indices of the hyperparameters used.
The E is for "Encoder" and the integer that follows indicates the number of classes.

EX: P1.0_E19 

There is also the checkpoint of pretrained encoder to start the training with.
